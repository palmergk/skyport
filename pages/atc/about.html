about us
Skyport, a professional air cargo Ground Handling Agent, currently operates at three locations in the Czech Republic (Prague airport) and Slovakia (Bratislava & Košice airports).
Established in 2007, Skyport today stands as one of the most successful GHAs in the Central European region.
Skyport offers innovative logistics and transportation solutions to over 40 global airlines, as well as mail services, airline catering and office rental space.
The state-of-the-art air cargo terminal at Vaclav Havel Airport Prague spans over 5 hectares, including a handling area and a non-public SRA zone with a stand for three wide-body freighters.
Our warehouse offers a cargo capacity of 200,000 tons and an additional dedicated mail center with 20,000 tons capacity. The SRA zone is equipped with an Elevating Transfer Vehicle for 220 ULDs - the only one in the Czech Republic and Slovakia, and amongst the very few in Central Europe.
With 170 highly experienced employees, Skyport provides world class quality service to all of its customers.
Our ambition is to expand our geographic coverage into more European markets to even better accommodate the service needs of our partners.
Skyport is a member of the International Air Cargo Association (TIACA).
