about the company
Skyport a.s. Czech Republic, Skyport Holding a.s. Czech Republic and Skyport s.r.o. Slovakia (hereinafter referred to as “Skyport”) operate from the most modern terminals in Europe Skyport is a reliable and technologically advanced partner in handling your cargo at airports in Prague, Bratislava and Košice. In combination with the truck centre and new technologies, Skyport's terminal complexes represent the real logistics centre for Central and Eastern Europe.
Skyport also provides ticket sales service at the airport offices in Bratislava and Košice.
where we carry out our business activity
prague
The cargo terminal’s operation at Vaclav Havel Prague Airport was launched in 2004. Since its inception in 2007, Skyport has been developing its activities and thanks to the modern technological equipment and professional approach of all team members, Skyport is today the leader in its field. Skyport offers a wide range of services for the convenience and benefit of business partners, services such as a fully automated warehouse for air pallets or spare air transport (RFS). Interested customers can also use ancillary services such as rental of office space and advertising space.


Skyport's clients include leading airlines such as CSA, Korean Air, Delta Airlines, China Airlines, Qatar Airlines, Hainan Airlines, Aeroflot, Finnair, China Eastern, Cargolux, Sichuan Airlines and many others.
bratislava
In 2008, Skyport expanded its operations abroad. The Slovak branch at M. R. Štefánik Airport in Bratislava was opened, offering quality services such as mail handling, customs clearance or ticket sales. Slovak Skyport has modern technologies, professional access to all customers and precise compliance with terms and conditions. That's why Skyport has built its reputation very quickly on the Slovak market as well.
Košice
In January 2011, another branch was opened in Slovakia at Košice - Barca Airport as part of Skyport's growth. Using modern technology, we provide our clients with professional services such as supervising check-in, passenger, cargo and ticket sales. Our services are used by many companies.
